About CropWise

Smart Agricultural Advisory Prototype

CropWise is a smart agricultural advisory platform developed by Msughter Sulega to help Lagos farmers choose suitable crops based on soil type, season, and location.

About Project

What CropWise Does

CropWise helps smallholder farmers in Lagos State make better planting decisions by recommending suitable crops based on the current season, soil type, water needs, expected yield, and location.

The platform supports farmers in Ikorodu, Epe, and Badagry LGAs. It includes a crop recommendation dashboard, crop library, crop detail pages, personal farm tracking, and offline-ready farm record support.

About Developer

Msughter Sulega

Msughter Sulega is a Computer Science Student and ML & IoT Enthusiast. He developed CropWise as an academic prototype to demonstrate how software, agricultural data, and smart advisory logic can support farmers.

CropWise v1.0 combines Flask, MySQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and rule-based crop recommendation logic to provide practical farming guidance.
