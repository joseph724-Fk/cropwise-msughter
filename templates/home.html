About Project

CropWise is built for smarter smallholder farming.

CropWise is a smart crop advisory and farm management platform designed to help farmers make better planting decisions. It recommends crops using seasonal timing, crop water needs, expected yield, and soil compatibility.

What CropWise Does

Seasonal Crop Advisory

Visitors immediately see the best crops to plant at the current time of the year. Registered farmers get more personal recommendations based on LGA and soil type.

Farm Management

Personal Crop Tracking

Farmers can record planted crops, planting dates, expected harvest dates, and crop status. Offline mode allows farm records to be saved locally and synced when internet returns.
